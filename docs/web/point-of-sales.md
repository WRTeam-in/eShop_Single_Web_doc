# Point of Sale (POS) System

The Point of Sale (POS) system enables staff to process in-store customer orders quickly and efficiently, combining product browsing, cart management, payment processing, and order fulfillment in one interface.

:::tip Key Benefits
- **Fast order processing** with intuitive interface
- **Multiple payment methods** (Cash, Card, QR Code, Net Banking, Online)
- **Flexible delivery options** (Local Pickup, Door-Step Delivery)
- **Real-time inventory tracking**
:::

---

## Accessing POS

1. Log in to your **Admin Panel**
2. Navigate to **POS** from the main menu
3. The POS interface will load with the product catalog

---

## POS Interface Overview

![Point of Sale Interface](/img/web/pos.png)

The POS system has three main sections:

### 1. **Product Catalog (Left Panel)**

**Category Filter:**
- Dropdown to filter products by category
- Located at top-left: "Filter Product By Category"

**Product Grid:**
- Displays products with images, names, prices, and variants
- Click **"Add to Cart"** to add items

**Adding Products:**
1. Browse or use category filter to find products
2. Select product variant if applicable (e.g., "2 gb - $ 900")
3. Click **"Add to Cart"**

### 2. **Customer Panel (Top-Right)**

**Existing Customer:**
- Use "Search for user" dropdown
- Search by name, email, or phone
- Select customer from results

**New Customer:**
- Click **"Register"** button (green)
- Fill in customer details (name, email, phone, address)
- Save to create new customer account

:::note Walk-in Customers
For customers who don't want to register, create a generic "Walk-in Customer" account or process as guest.
:::

### 3. **Shopping Cart (Right Panel)**

**Cart Management:**
- **Product Image & Name:** Visual reference
- **Price:** Unit price per item
- **Quantity Controls:** Use **+/−** buttons to adjust
- **Remove :** Delete item from cart

**Order Summary:**
- **Subtotal:** Sum of all items
- **Final Total:** Total amount to pay (highlighted in red)

**Delivery Type:**
- **Local Pickup:** Customer collects from store (no delivery charge)
- **Door-Step Delivery:** Delivered to address (may include charges)

**Payment Methods:**
1. **Cash** - Physical currency
2. **Card Payment** - Credit/debit card via terminal
3. **Net Banking** - Direct bank transfer
4. **Online Payment** - Payment gateway
5. **Other** - Store credit, vouchers, etc.

**Action Buttons:**
- Buy Now - Complete order and process payment
- Clear Cart - Remove all items
