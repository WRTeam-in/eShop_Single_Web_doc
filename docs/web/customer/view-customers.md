## Customers Overview

![View Customers](/img/web/view_customer.png)

The **View Customers** page allows administrators to view and manage all registered customers on the platform.  
It provides essential customer information, account status, and quick access to customer-related actions.

---

## Customer List Details

Each customer record in the list displays the following information:

- **ID**  
  A unique system-generated identifier for the customer.

- **Name**  
  The full name of the customer as provided during registration.

- **Email**  
  The registered email address used for login and communication.

- **Mobile No**  
  The customer’s primary contact number.

- **Image**  
  The customer’s profile image.  
  If no image is uploaded, a default placeholder is displayed.

- **Balance**  
  The current wallet balance available for the customer.

- **Street**  
  Street-level address details.

- **Area**  
  Area or locality associated with the customer’s address.

- **City**  
  City name linked to the customer profile.

- **Date**  
  The date on which the customer account was created.

- **Status**  
  Indicates whether the customer account is active or inactive.

- **Actions**  
  A dropdown menu providing customer-related management options.

---

## Customer Actions

From the **Actions** menu, administrators can perform the following operations:

- **View Orders**  
  View all orders placed by the selected customer.

- **View Transactions**  
  Access the customer’s wallet and payment transaction history.

- **View Address**  
  View all saved addresses associated with the customer.

- **Enable / Disable**  
  Activate or deactivate the customer account.

---

## Search & Pagination

The customer list includes built-in tools to manage large datasets efficiently:

- **Search**  
  Search customers quickly using their **name** or **email address**.

- **Pagination**  
  Navigate through multiple pages when customer records exceed the page limit.

- **Rows Per Page**  
  Control how many customer records are displayed on a single page.

---

## Status Control

Customer account status affects platform access:

- **Active Customers**
  - Can log in to the platform
  - Can place orders
  - Can access wallet and services

- **Disabled Customers**
  - Cannot log in
  - Cannot place orders
  - Platform access is restricted

---



:::note Supported Formats
- Customer information is displayed in real time based on the database.
- Any changes made using the **Actions** menu take effect immediately.
- Only authorized administrators can manage customer accounts.
:::
