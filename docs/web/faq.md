# FAQ Management

FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) Management allows you to create and manage a collection of common questions and answers that help customers find information quickly without contacting support.

:::tip Why Use FAQs?
- **Reduce support workload** by answering common questions
- **Improve customer experience** with self-service help
- **Provide instant answers** 24/7
- **Build trust** by addressing concerns proactively
- **Improve SEO** with relevant content
:::

---

## FAQ Dashboard

![FAQ List](/img/web/faq_list.jpg)

The FAQ dashboard displays all created FAQs with their questions, answers, and status.

**Table Information:**
- **Question**: The FAQ question text
- **Answer**: Brief preview of the answer
- **Status**: Active (visible on website) or Inactive (hidden)
- **Created Date**: When the FAQ was added
- **Actions**: Quick action buttons

---


## Adding a New FAQ

### Step 1: Click "Add FAQ"

Click the **"Add FAQ"** button located on the right side of the dashboard.

### Step 2: Fill FAQ Form

![Add FAQ](/img/web/add_faq.jpg)

A form will appear with the following fields:

#### **1. Question**
- Enter the frequently asked question
- **Tips**:
  - Write questions as customers would ask them
  - Keep questions clear and concise
  - Use natural language
  - Start with question words (What, How, When, Why, Can)

**Examples:**
- "How do I track my order?"
- "What is your return policy?"
- "Do you ship internationally?"
- "How long does delivery take?"

#### **2. Answer**
- Provide a clear, helpful answer to the question
- **Tips**:
  - Be specific and detailed
  - Use simple language
  - Include step-by-step instructions if needed
  - Add links to relevant pages
  - Keep answers concise but complete

**Example Answer:**
```
You can track your order by:
1. Logging into your account
2. Going to "My Orders"
3. Clicking on the order number
4. Viewing the tracking information

You will also receive tracking updates via email.
```

#### **3. Status**
- **Active**: FAQ is visible on the website
- **Inactive**: FAQ is hidden from customers

**When to use Inactive:**
- Preparing FAQs for future publication
- Temporarily hiding outdated information
- Testing new FAQ content
