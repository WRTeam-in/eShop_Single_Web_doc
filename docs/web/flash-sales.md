# Flash Sales Management

Flash Sales are time-limited promotional campaigns that offer special discounts on selected products to create urgency and boost sales. This powerful marketing tool helps you drive customer engagement and increase revenue through strategic, limited-time offers.

:::tip Marketing Strategy
Flash Sales are highly effective for:
- **Clearing inventory** of seasonal or slow-moving products
- **Boosting sales** during specific periods or events
- **Attracting new customers** with compelling limited-time offers
- **Increasing customer engagement** and creating urgency
- **Testing price sensitivity** for different product categories
:::

---

## Flash Sales Overview

The Flash Sales feature allows administrators to create and manage promotional campaigns with:

- **Time-bound discounts** with specific start and end dates
- **Product-specific promotions** targeting individual items
- **Percentage-based discounts** to incentivize purchases
- **Visual banners** to highlight ongoing sales on the website
- **Automated activation/deactivation** based on scheduled timings

## Accessing Flash Sales Management

**Navigation Path:**

1. Log in to your **Admin Panel**
2. Navigate to  **Flash Sales** 
3. You'll see the Flash Sales management dashboard

**What you can do:**
- View all active and scheduled flash sales
- Create new flash sale campaigns
- Edit existing flash sales
- Delete or deactivate campaigns

## Creating a New Flash Sale

### Step 1: Click "Add Flash Sale"

On the Flash Sales management page, click the **"Add Flash Sale"** or **"Create New"** button to begin creating a new campaign.

### Step 2: Configure Flash Sale Details

Fill in the following required information:

#### **1. Flash Sale Title**
- Enter a descriptive name for your campaign
- **Example:** "Summer Clearance Sale", "Weekend Special Offer", "New Year Mega Sale"
- This title helps you identify the campaign in the admin panel

:::note Best Practice
Use clear, descriptive titles that indicate the campaign period or theme for easy management.
:::

#### **2. Select Products**
- Choose which products will be included in this flash sale
- You can select:
  - **Individual products** from your catalog
  - **Multiple products** at once
  - Products from specific categories (if supported)

**How to select products:**
1. Click the **"Select Products"** dropdown or button
2. Browse or search for products
3. Check the boxes next to products you want to include
4. Confirm your selection

:::warning Important
Only **active and published products** should be included in flash sales. Ensure selected products have sufficient stock.
:::

#### **3. Discount Percentage**
- Enter the discount amount as a percentage (e.g., 10%, 25%, 50%)
- **Valid range:** Typically 1% to 99%
- This discount will be applied to the original product price

**Discount Calculation Example:**
- Original Price: $100
- Discount: 25%
- **Flash Sale Price: $75**

:::tip Pricing Strategy
- **10-20% discount:** Good for premium products or regular promotions
- **25-40% discount:** Effective for seasonal clearance or mid-range campaigns
- **50%+ discount:** Best for inventory clearance or major sales events
:::

#### **4. Start Date & Time**
- Select when the flash sale should begin
- Use the date/time picker to set the exact start moment
- The sale will automatically activate at this time

**Considerations:**
- Schedule sales during **peak shopping hours** for maximum impact
- Consider **time zones** if serving international customers
- Allow sufficient time for **marketing preparation** before launch

#### **5. End Date & Time**
- Select when the flash sale should end
- The sale will automatically deactivate at this time
- Products will revert to their original prices

**Duration Recommendations:**
- **24-48 hours:** Creates strong urgency, ideal for flash campaigns
- **3-7 days:** Good for weekend or weekly promotions
- **1-2 weeks:** Suitable for seasonal or clearance sales

:::caution Time Management
Ensure the end date is **after** the start date. The system may prevent overlapping flash sales on the same products.
:::

#### **6. Upload Banner Image**
- Upload an attractive promotional banner image
- This banner will be displayed on your website's Flash Sale section

**Banner Specifications:**
- **Recommended dimensions:** 1200x400px or 1920x600px (check your theme)
- **File format:** JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **File size:** Keep under 500KB for optimal loading speed
- **Design tips:**
  - Use bold, eye-catching colors (orange, red, yellow work well)
  - Include clear text: "SPECIAL DISCOUNT", "Upto X% OFF"
  - Add product images if highlighting specific items
  - Ensure text is readable on mobile devices



:::tip Design Resources
Use tools like **Canva**, **Adobe Express**, or **Figma** to create professional flash sale banners quickly.
:::

#### **7. Status**
- Set the flash sale status:
  - **Active:** Sale is live (if within scheduled time)
  - **Inactive:** Sale is disabled (won't show even during scheduled time)
  - **Scheduled:** Sale will activate automatically at start time

### Step 3: Save Flash Sale

After filling in all details:
1. Review all information for accuracy
2. Click **"Save"** or **"Create Flash Sale"**
3. You'll see a success message confirming creation
4. The flash sale will appear in your management dashboard

## Managing Existing Flash Sales

### Viewing Flash Sales List

The Flash Sales dashboard displays all campaigns with:
- **Flash Sale Title**
- **Discount Percentage**
- **Start Date & End Date**
- **Status** (Active, Scheduled, Expired, Inactive)
- **Number of Products** included
- **Action buttons** (Edit, Delete, View)

### Editing a Flash Sale

**To modify an existing flash sale:**

1. Locate the flash sale in the list
2. Click the **"Edit"** icon/button
3. Update any of the following:
   - Title
   - Products (add/remove)
   - Discount percentage
   - Start/End dates
   - Banner image
   - Status
4. Click **"Update"** to save changes

:::warning Active Sales
Be cautious when editing **active flash sales** as changes take effect immediately and may affect customer purchases in progress.
:::

### Deleting a Flash Sale

**To remove a flash sale:**

1. Click the **"Delete"** icon/button next to the flash sale
2. Confirm the deletion in the popup dialog
3. The flash sale will be permanently removed

:::danger Caution
Deleting a flash sale is **permanent** and cannot be undone. Consider setting status to "Inactive" instead if you might need it later.
:::

### Activating/Deactivating Flash Sales

**Quick Status Toggle:**
- Use the **status toggle switch** to quickly activate or deactivate a flash sale
- **Active:** Sale is visible and functional (if within scheduled time)
- **Inactive:** Sale is hidden from customers

## Flash Sale Display on Website

### Customer-Facing Features

When a flash sale is active, customers will see:

1. **Flash Sale Banner**
   - Displayed prominently on the homepage or dedicated Flash Sales page
   - Shows the uploaded banner image
   - Includes discount information and call-to-action

2. **Discounted Prices**
   - Original price shown with strikethrough
   - Flash sale price displayed prominently
   - Discount percentage badge (e.g., "25% OFF")

3. **Countdown Timer** (if implemented)
   - Shows time remaining until sale ends
   - Creates urgency and encourages immediate purchase

4. **Flash Sale Badge**
   - Products in flash sales display special badges
   - Helps customers identify promotional items quickly
