# Manage Products

The **Manage Products** module is the central hub for overseeing your entire product catalog.  
From here, administrators can view, search, edit, and delete products, as well as initiate the process to add new inventory items.

![Manage Products Page Overview](/img/web/manage_pro_tab.jpg)

---

## Overview

The Product Management interface is designed to provide a comprehensive view of your inventory with powerful tools to maintain it.

**Key Capabilities:**
- **Centralized Listing**: View all products in a single, sortable table.
- **Quick Actions**: Rapidly edit details or remove outdated items.
- **Advanced Filtering**: Drill down to specific products using search and filter options.

---

## Product List Management

The main content area displays the product registry.

### Control Toolbar

- **Search** – Locate products by Name, SKU, or other identifiers
- **Refresh** – Reload the list to see real-time inventory updates
- **Filter** – Apply specific criteria to narrow down the product list

### Product Actions

In the product table, each row provides actionable controls:

- **Edit**  
  Click the **Edit** button to modify product details such as price, stock, or description.

- **Delete**  
  Click the **Delete** button to permanently remove a product from the catalog.

:::caution
Deleting a product is irreversible. Ensure the product is not part of any active orders before removal.
:::

## Add New Product

To create a new product, click the **Add Product** button located on the right side of the toolbar.

:::info Detailed Guide
For a step-by-step walkthrough of the product creation process, including advanced configuration and variable products, please refer to the **[Add Products Guide](./add-products.md)**.
:::

![Add Product Form](/img/web/add_product_tab.jpg)

### Configuration Fields

When adding a product, you will need to populate the following key information:

#### 1. Basic Information
- **Product Name**: The clear, public-facing title of the item.
- **Description**: A detailed summary of the product features and benefits.
- **Made In**: The country of origin (e.g., "India", "USA").

#### 2. Categorization & Attributes
- **Category**: Assign the product to a specific category for better organization.
- **Indicator**: Specify dietary or usage type if applicable (e.g., **Veg**, **Non-Veg**).

#### 3. Pricing & Tax
- **Tax**: Select the applicable tax rule for this product.

#### 4. Media
High-quality images are crucial for sales.

:::info
For detailed instructions on uploading and managing product images, refer to the [Media Management](/docs/web/media-management.md) guide.
:::

### Finalizing Creation

- **Reset** – Clears all entered data to start fresh.
- **Add Product** – Saves the product to the database.
