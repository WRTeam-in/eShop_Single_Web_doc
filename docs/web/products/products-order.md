# Product Display Order

The **Products Order** page allows you to customize the sequence in which products appear on your storefront.  
By adjusting the order here, you can prioritize best-sellers, new arrivals, or specific items you wish to highlight to your customers.

![Products Order Interface](/img/web/products_order.jpg)

---

## Overview

Controlling the display order is a powerful merchandising tool. It ensures that customers see your most important products first when browsing categories or search results.

**Key Functionality:**
- **Reorder Products**: Change the visual sequence of items.
- **Immediate Update**: Changes are synced instantly to the app/website.

---

## How to Check Product Order

1.  Navigate to **Products > Products Order**.
2.  You will see a list of products currently active in the store.
3.  **Update the Sequence**:
    - Depending on the interface, you may either **drag and drop** rows or enter a numeric **Rank/Sort Order** for each item.
    - Lower numbers usually appear first (e.g., 1, 2, 3...).

:::info Impact on Storefront
**Important**: Any changes made to the order on this page will be **directly reflected on the customer-facing product page**.
As soon as you save the new order, products will be rearranged on the website and mobile app accordingly.
:::
