# Home Slider Images

Home Slider Images are the rotating banner images displayed prominently on your website's homepage. These eye-catching visuals help showcase featured products, promotions, and important announcements to visitors.

:::tip Why Use Sliders?
- **Grab attention** with dynamic, rotating visuals
- **Showcase multiple promotions** in one space
- **Highlight featured products** or collections
- **Drive engagement** with clickable banners
- **Create visual appeal** on your homepage
:::

---

## Slider Management Dashboard

![Home Slider Tab](/img/web/home_slider_tab.jpg)

The slider dashboard displays all configured slider images with the following information:

**Table Columns:**
- **Preview**: Thumbnail of the slider image
- **Type**: Category or purpose of the slider
- **Status**: Active or Inactive
- **Created Date**: When the slider was added
- **Actions**: Quick action buttons

---



## Adding a New Slider

### Step 1: Click "Add Slider"

Click the **"Add Slider"** button located on the right side of the dashboard.

### Step 2: Fill Slider Form

![Home Slider Form](/img/web/home_slider3_tab.jpg)

A form will appear with the following fields:

#### **1. Type**
- Enter the type or category of the slider
- **Examples**: "Promotional", "Featured Product", "Seasonal Sale"
- Helps organize and identify sliders

#### **2. Upload Image**
- Click the **"Upload Image"** button
- Select image from media library or upload new
- **Recommended size**: 1920x600px or 1200x400px
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP

:::tip Image Upload
For detailed instructions on uploading images, refer to the [Media Management](/docs/web/media-management) guide.
:::

#### **3. Link (Optional)**
- Add a URL where users will be redirected when clicking the slider
- **Example**: Product page, category page, or promotional landing page

#### **4. Status**
- Set slider to **Active** (visible on website) or **Inactive** (hidden)

### Step 3: Save Slider

- Click **"Add Slider"** to save the new slider
- Click **"Reset"** to clear the form and start over
- The new slider will appear in the slider list

## Reflecting on Frontend

The Home Slider management system allows you to:

- **Add** new slider images with custom content
- **Edit** existing slider images and settings

![Slider on Website](/img/web/slider-web.png)

**How it appears to customers:**
- Sliders rotate automatically on the homepage
- Visitors can click on sliders to navigate to linked pages
- Navigation dots or arrows allow manual slider control
- Only **Active** sliders are visible to customers
- Sliders display in the order configured
