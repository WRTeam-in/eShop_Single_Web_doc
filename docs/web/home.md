## Overview

The **Home Panel** serves as the central command center for your eShop administration. It provides an at-a-glance view of your business performance, key metrics, and quick access to essential management tools.

![Dashboard Overview](/img/web/admin-home.png)

### Key Metrics
The dashboard highlights four critical areas of your business:

- **Orders**: View comprehensive information about customer orders.
- **New Signups**: Track new user registrations and growth.
- **Delivery Boys**: Manage and view information regarding your delivery fleet.
- **Products**: Access your product catalog and inventory details.

### Performance Analytics
Visual charts provide insights into your sales trends:

- **Product Sales**: Summarizes sales performance over the daily, weekly, and monthly periods.
- **Category Wise Product**: Displays the distribution of products across different categories.

---

## Theme Customization

You can personalize the look and feel of the admin panel using the customization tools found on the side panel. Options include:

*   **Visual Style**: Toggle borders, adjust text sizes (Body, Navbar, Sidebar, Footer), and enable flat styles for navigation.
*   **Layout**: Configure sidebar indentation and responsive behaviors.
*   **Color Variants**: Choose between various Navbar and Dark Sidebar variants to match your branding preferences.

---

## User Profile Management

The header section provides quick access to your account settings.

![Header Users](/img/web/header-users.jpg)

From the profile menu, you can:

- **Profile**: Update administrator details and account security settings.
- **Log Out**: Securely sign out of the admin panel.
