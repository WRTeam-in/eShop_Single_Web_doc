# Offers Management

Offers Management allows you to create and display promotional offer banners on your website. These visual offers help attract customer attention to special deals, discounts, and limited-time promotions.

:::tip Why Use Offer Images?
- **Promote special deals** with eye-catching visuals
- **Highlight discounts** and limited-time offers
- **Drive conversions** with targeted promotions
- **Increase engagement** on product pages
- **Showcase seasonal sales** effectively
:::

---

## Offers Dashboard

![Offers Tab](/img/web/offers_tab.jpg)

The offers dashboard displays all configured offer images with management tools.

**Table Information:**
- **Preview**: Thumbnail of the offer image
- **Status**: Active or Inactive
- **Created Date**: When the offer was added
- **Actions**: Quick action buttons

---

## Adding a New Offer

### Step 1: Click "Add Offer"

Click the **"Add Offer"** button located on the right side of the dashboard.

![Offers Form](/img/web/offers_tab2.jpg)

### Step 2: Fill Offer Form

![Offers Details](/img/web/offers_tab3.jpg)

A form will appear for you to configure the offer:

#### **Upload Offer Image**
- Click the **"Upload Image"** button
- Select image from media library or upload new
- **Recommended size**: 800x400px or 600x300px
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP

:::tip Image Upload
For detailed instructions on uploading images, refer to the [Media Management](/docs/web/media-management) guide.
:::

#### **Link (Optional)**
- Add a URL where users will be redirected when clicking the offer
- **Example**: Product page, category page, or promotional landing page

#### **Status**
- Set offer to **Active** (visible on website) or **Inactive** (hidden)

### Step 3: Save Offer

- Click **"Add Offer"** to save the new offer
- Click **"Reset"** to clear the form and start over
- The new offer will appear in the offers list
